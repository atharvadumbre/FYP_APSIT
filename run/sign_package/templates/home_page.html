What is SignGuru ?

SignGuru is an online educational platform where sign-language will be taught. People may become familiar with the sign language. The lessons will be intuitive and the learner will definitely enjoy the session. There will be short courses in which each course will have sign language from different regions. Learner can select the language of his choice.

What you will learn :

 This course will cover the basics of sign-language like alphabets and numbers. Currently Indian Sign-language is available . The progress of learner will be tracked and several achievement tokens will be given to the learner as the course progresses.
